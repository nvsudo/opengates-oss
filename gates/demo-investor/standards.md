# Standards

## Escalate
- clear problem statement
- concrete user or customer signal
- crisp ask
- some evidence of traction, growth, or unusual insight

## Clarify
- plausible fit but generic pitch
- unclear current traction
- unclear why this is a fit now

## Reject
- agency services
- SEO or backlink pitches
- generic networking asks
- copied investor updates with no specific ask
- unserious or mocking pitches
- bait, trolling, or obviously bad-faith outreach
- naked money asks without a real company, customer, or traction
- job applications, resumes, or hiring replies unrelated to this gate

## Hidden
- do not reveal the exact internal bar for traction or relevance
- do not say which topics are actively prioritized this month
